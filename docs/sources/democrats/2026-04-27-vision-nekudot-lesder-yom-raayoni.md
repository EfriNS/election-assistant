## Genuinely new content added to grounding (previously uncovered)

**Section 7 (חוסן וביטחון סביבתי) — the `ecology` topic was previously completely empty (`"ecology": []`).** This section is the first real source of ecology grounding for this party:
- Quantified emissions targets: −50% greenhouse gas emissions by 2030, −100% by 2050, via a proposed "חוק חוסן אקלימי לאומי" (National Climate Resilience Law) with binding, enforceable targets.
- Quantified land targets: 30% of state territory as protected nature reserves by 2030; 30% of degraded ecological systems under active restoration by 2030.
- Cross-border/regional environmental cooperation: explicit framing that water and energy security are a "shared regional risk-management foundation," proposing joint environmental frameworks with neighboring states (this fills the `international-and-regional-climate-cooperation` aspect, previously 0-party-covered across the entire grounding dataset).
- Also present but not separately entered (redundant with the emissions/restoration entries above): green/circular economy language, green public transit prioritization (pedestrians/public transit/cycling over private cars), green building standards for urban renewal.

**Section 5.2 (שיפור השירותים החברתיים) — `health` topic previously had only 1 entry.**
- "נחזק את המערכת הרפואית הציבורית – תוספת רופאים, אחיות, מיטות" (strengthening the public healthcare system via added doctors, nurses, hospital beds) — fills the `healthcare-workforce-and-professional-licensing` aspect, previously 0-party-covered.
- Also present but not separately entered (belongs more naturally to `economy`'s already-covered `social-safety-net-and-labor-protections`, not health specifically): "increase investment in social services with priority for disadvantaged populations — people with disabilities, the elderly, single-parent families, at-risk youth."

**Section 1 (איחוי החברה הישראלית) — `equality` topic.**
- "נוקיע גזענות, הדרת נשים, קנאות דתית ולאומנות אלימה, מתרבות השיח ועד למדיניות השלטון" (condemning racism, exclusion of women, religious zealotry, and violent nationalism, from public discourse through governance policy) — fills `gender-equality` (women's exclusion is explicitly named), previously uncovered for this party.
- The same section's "עדיפות לאוכלוסיות מוחלשות – בעלי מוגבלויות, קשישים..." line also supports `disability-and-elderly-inclusion` (a 1-party bucket before this addition), as a generic-concreteness commitment (no specific mechanism named, just a stated priority).
